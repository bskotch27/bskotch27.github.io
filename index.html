About

I am a PhD candidate in Management of Organizations at the Haas School of Business, UC Berkeley. My research sits at the intersection of innovation, organizations, and technology — with a focus on how AI tools reshape knowledge production and how institutional transitions affect inventor performance. Before academia, I worked in product management and public policy across India and Europe.
